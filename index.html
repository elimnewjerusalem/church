About Us

We exist to
worship God
& serve His people.

Elim New Jerusalem Church, Tondiarpet is a spirit-filled congregation committed to worship, prayer, and deliverance ministry. Founded by Pastor A. Karthik Daniel, we believe in the power of God to heal, restore and set the captives free.
