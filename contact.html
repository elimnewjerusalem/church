First Time?

Plan Your Visit

1
Pick a service time
We have three Sunday services — 5:30 AM, 8:30 AM, and 12:00 PM. All are in Tamil. Come to whichever time works for you.
2
Find us easily
No. 110E, Elaya Street, Tondiarpet — near Bethastha Swimming Pool. Google Maps link below will take you right to the door.
3
Come as you are
No dress code. No prior experience needed. Just bring yourself — God's house is open to everyone regardless of background.
4
What to expect
Worship in Tamil, a message from God's Word, prayer time, and a warm welcome from our congregation. Services are 90–120 minutes.

Children

Sunday School runs alongside the main service — 6:30 to 9:30 AM every Sunday. Children are always welcome in the main service too.

Questions?

We're happy to answer any questions before your visit.
