About Our Church

Building a community of faith, hope, and love

Our Story

Elim New Jerusalem Church (ENJC) is a vibrant community of believers dedicated to spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ. Founded on the principles of worship, fellowship, and service, we strive to create an environment where everyone can encounter God's presence and grow in their faith journey.

Our mission is to make disciples, build strong families, and impact our community through the love of Christ. We believe in the power of prayer, the authority of God's Word, and the transforming work of the Holy Spirit.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be a church that glorifies God by making disciples who love Jesus, live in community, and serve the world with compassion and excellence.

Our Mission

Empowering believers to grow spiritually, connect meaningfully, and serve sacrificially through worship, discipleship, and outreach.
