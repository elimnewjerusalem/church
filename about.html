Our Story

Elim New Jerusalem Church was founded with a vision to bring the transforming power of Jesus Christ to families and communities. What started as a small gathering of believers has grown into a vibrant church family dedicated to worship, fellowship, and service.

We believe in the power of prayer, the authority of God's Word, and the presence of the Holy Spirit. Our church is built on a foundation of faith, hope, and love, where every person is welcomed and valued.

Today, we continue to grow as a community of believers, united in our mission to glorify God and share His love with the world.

Pastor's Testimony

"God called me out of darkness into His marvelous light. When I surrendered my life to Christ, He transformed my heart and gave me a vision to plant a church where broken lives could be restored. Elim New Jerusalem Church is a testimony of God's faithfulness. Every soul saved, every family restored, and every life transformed is evidence that our God is alive and working among us. This ministry is not about me — it's about Jesus Christ and His redemptive power."

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To glorify God by proclaiming the Gospel of Jesus Christ, making disciples, and equipping believers to live out their faith through worship, fellowship, and service to others.

Our Vision

To be a Christ-centered community where lives are transformed, families are strengthened, and the love of Jesus reaches every generation and every nation.
