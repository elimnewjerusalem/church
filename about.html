Our Ministry

About Our Deliverance Ministry

We believe in the power of Jesus Christ to set people free — from bondage, sickness, and spiritual oppression. Our deliverance ministry is rooted in prayer, God's Word, and the presence of the Holy Spirit.

🙏

Prayer & Intercession

Daily prayer meetings and special intercession gatherings. Every believer is invited to pray for families, Chennai, and nations.

📖

Word & Worship

Grounded in the authority of Scripture. We preach, teach, and live God's Word every week through three Sunday services.

🕊

Holy Spirit Ministry

We welcome the gifts and move of the Holy Spirit. Healing, deliverance, and transformation happen when God's people gather.

Mission

Our Mission

To bring the transforming power of Jesus Christ to families and communities in Tondiarpet, Chennai, and beyond — through worship, prayer, the Word of God, and Spirit-led ministry.

  • Reach the lost
    Proclaim the Gospel in Tondiarpet and all of Chennai
  • Strengthen believers
    Build up every member in faith, prayer, and God's Word
  • Transform families
    See whole households saved, healed, and set free

Vision

Our Vision

Starting in Chennai and expanding across all of Tamil Nadu and beyond — to every village and nation where Jesus has not yet been named.

  • 🌍
    Expand church branches worldwide
    Establish ENJC branches globally
  • 🔥
    Establish deliverance ministries
    Spirit-led deliverance centres across Tamil Nadu
  • 🧑
    Reach young people with Jesus
    Preach the Gospel to the next generation
  • 🏘
    Village expansion across Tamil Nadu
    Starting in Chennai — reaching every Tamil Nadu village
