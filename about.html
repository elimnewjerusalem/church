Our Pastor

Senior Pastor
Pastor A. Karthik Daniel
💬 Contact Pastor ▶ Watch Sermons

Pastor A. Karthik Daniel leads Elim New Jerusalem Church with a God-given vision for deliverance, prayer, and reaching the lost across Chennai and Tamil Nadu. Called by God from a young age, he has faithfully served the congregation at Tondiarpet with humility, faith, and fire.

He is deeply committed to the Word of God, the transforming power of the Holy Spirit, and seeing families — whole households — saved, healed, and set free in Jesus' name. His ministry spans Sunday services, Friday prayer meetings, daily night prayer, and special deliverance and Promise services.

Our Journey

Church History

1989
🌱

Church Founded

Elim New Jerusalem Church was established in Tondiarpet, Chennai — a small gathering with a great vision: to bring the power of Jesus Christ to the people of North Chennai.

2000s
🔥

Deliverance Ministry Grows

God's power moved mightily through the Deliverance Ministry. Families were set free, healings took place, and the congregation grew as testimonies spread across Chennai.

2010s
📖

Multiple Services & Outreach

Sunday services expanded to three gatherings to accommodate the growing congregation. Promise Services, Testimony nights, and Tamil Nadu village outreaches began.

2020
📡

Live Streaming Begins

ENJC took the Gospel online — live streaming Sunday services and night prayer meetings on YouTube, reaching believers across India and around the world.

Today
✝️

Still Pressing Forward

Three Sunday services · Friday Family Blessing Prayer · Daily Night Prayer at 10:30 PM · Bible Reader app · Reaching Tamil Nadu and the nations for Jesus Christ.

Our Ministry

About Our Deliverance Ministry

We believe in the power of Jesus Christ to set people free — from bondage, sickness, and spiritual oppression. Our deliverance ministry is rooted in prayer, God's Word, and the presence of the Holy Spirit.

🙏

Prayer & Intercession

Daily prayer meetings and special intercession gatherings. Every believer is invited to pray for families, Chennai, and nations.

📖

Word & Worship

Grounded in the authority of Scripture. We preach, teach, and live God's Word every week through three Sunday services.

🕊

Holy Spirit Ministry

We welcome the gifts and move of the Holy Spirit. Healing, deliverance, and transformation happen when God's people gather.

Mission
