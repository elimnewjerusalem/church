About Our Church

Welcome to Elim New Jerusalem Church

Elim New Jerusalem Church (ENJC) is a Christ-centered family of believers located in Tondiarpet, Chennai. Our heart is to worship God, grow in His Word, and serve people with love.

We exist to share the Gospel of Jesus Christ and help every person experience His grace, healing, and salvation.

Message by Our Pastor

Pr. A. Karthik Daniel

"Jesus Christ is the foundation and leader of our church. Through His teachings, love, and sacrifice, He guides our hearts, our mission, and our community. Everything we do is centered on glorifying Him."
