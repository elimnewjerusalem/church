Outreach

Village Reach

ENJC teams travel every month to villages across Tamil Nadu — preaching the Gospel, praying for the sick, and planting the Word of God. This is the beating heart of our outreach ministry.

Sisters' Village Ministry goes out on the 3rd Saturday every month. Brothers' Village Ministry goes out on the 4th Saturday. Every Sunday afternoon at 3 PM, the Gospel Ministry team takes the Word to the streets of Chennai.
